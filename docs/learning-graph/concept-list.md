This is the enumerated list of concepts for the Networking and Communication intelligent textbook. Each concept has a unique ConceptID (the line number) and a Title-Case label of at most 32 characters. The concepts are organized roughly from foundational to advanced and span the full course breadth — architecture, physical/link layers, LANs, network and routing, transport, applications, security, wireless, programming, measurement, operations, SDN/NFV, emerging topics, and capstone projects.
