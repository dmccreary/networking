# Mascot Style Guide

This page shows all of Buzz's admonition styles for reference. Use it after generating the mascot images to verify that every pose loads, the colors match the book's theme, and text wrapping looks clean.

!!! mascot-neutral "A Note from Buzz"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/neutral.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Buzz neutral pose">
    This is the **neutral** style, used for general sidebars or asides that don't call for a specific emotional tone.

!!! mascot-welcome "Welcome to the Chapter!"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/welcome.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Buzz waving welcome">
    This is the **welcome** style, used at the opening of every chapter. *"Let's trace the route!"*

!!! mascot-thinking "Key Insight"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/thinking.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Buzz thinking">
    This is the **thinking** style, used to highlight key concepts and "aha" moments — for example, the end-to-end principle, or why CIDR replaced classful addressing.

!!! mascot-tip "Buzz's Tip"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/tip.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Buzz giving a tip">
    This is the **tip** style, used for practical hints — for example, *"Always run `tcpdump` with `-n` first to skip DNS lookups."*

!!! mascot-warning "Common Mistake"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/warning.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Buzz warning">
    This is the **warning** style, used for common mistakes — for example, confusing a subnet mask with a network address, or forgetting that NAT breaks end-to-end addressability.

!!! mascot-encourage "You've Got This!"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/encouraging.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Buzz encouraging">
    This is the **encouraging** style, used in difficult sections — subnetting practice, sliding-window math, or the first time a student writes a socket program.

!!! mascot-celebration "Great Progress!"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/celebration.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Buzz celebrating">
    This is the **celebration** style, used at the end of major sections or chapters to recognize accomplishment.
